PixICAM - Decentralised Pixel Place

PixIcam is a decentralized pixel place project based on a smart contract written in Solidity and deployed on the Goerli Ethereum network. Users can interact with the platform through a decentralized application (dApp) developed with JavaScript and Ether.js . To place pixels on the collaborative online canvas, users must pay a fee in GoerliETH.

Github repo : https://github.com/AxelMrsq/Pix-Icam↗

PixelPlace not only offers a unique collaborative art experience but also provides an opportunity for users to develop valuable skills. Participants will gain hands-on experience with blockchain technology and smart contracts, learning how decentralized systems operate.

Indeed, the project was part of a computer science competition at my school, which allowed me to win the honorary award for the most technically advanced project. The goal was to introduce my classmates to the world of blockchain, showcasing its potential and applications through an engaging and interactive platform.
